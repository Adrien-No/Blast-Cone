:arrow_right:  **general commands**

> .helplease | **displays this menu.**

:arrow_right:  **pre-game informations**

> **.tips (summonername) [server] [language] ** | Send tips to play against and with champs in summonername's game

example of using:
.tips yasuomain
.tips yasuomain euw fr_FR

note 1 : Parameters in brackets `[]` are optionnal (default : server=euw, language=fr_FR).
note 2 : Summonernames are case-insensitive and DO NOT REQUIRE SPACES " "
